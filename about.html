About Us

Welcome to Lykke Good, a haven for mid-century modern enthusiasts since our establishment in 1998.
We take pride in curating a collection that blends the timeless elegance of classic design with the vibrancy of contemporary aesthetics.
Our mission is simple: to bring joy and sophistication to your living spaces through furniture that transcends time.

Our Story

In the lively year of 1998, our founders, Julie, Nancy, and Slater, embarked on a shared vision to bring the beauty of mid-century modern furniture into the homes of those who appreciate the perfect fusion of fun and classic design. Alongside them is the heart and soul of our team, Pumpkin, a four-legged friend with a passion for comfort and style.

Embracing Timeless Design

At Lykke Good, we believe in the enduring allure of mid-century modern furniture. Each piece in our collection reflects the iconic styles of the past, celebrating the simplicity, functionality, and sleek lines that define this design era. Yet, we infuse a fresh perspective into every creation, ensuring that our furniture seamlessly integrates into contemporary lifestyles.

Meet the Team

Julie - The Visionary

Julie, the visionary behind Lykke Good, brings a keen eye for design and a love for all things mid-century. Her passion for curating unique pieces has been the driving force behind our collection. With an innate sense of style, Julie ensures that every item in our showroom reflects the essence of mid-century elegance.

Pumpkin - The Furry Muse

Meet Pumpkin, our beloved four-legged muse. With a wagging tail and a discerning eye for comfort, Pumpkin adds a touch of playfulness to our workspace. As our official stress-relief expert, Pumpkin ensures that every piece we offer is as cozy as it is stylish.

Nancy - The Detail-Oriented Perfectionist

Nancy, the detail-oriented perfectionist, oversees the quality and craftsmanship of each item we showcase. Her meticulous approach ensures that every piece meets the highest standards of excellence. From material selection to finishing touches, Nancy ensures that our furniture is a testament to quality and durability.

Slater - The Creative Soul

Slater, the creative soul of Lykke Good, brings innovation and imagination to our design process. His artistic flair and inventive ideas contribute to the playful and modern elements infused into our classic pieces. Slater's creativity ensures that our furniture resonates with a diverse range of tastes and preferences.
